\begin{abstract}
This note is a \emph{speculative bridge}: it maps science-fiction tropes (foldspace engines, navigators, prescience) onto the \emph{notation} of MQGT--SCF---real scalar fields $\Phi_c(x)$ and $E(x)$, a local GKSL measurement container, and optional measure-level tilts---without claiming Dune continuity as physics canon and without asserting new experimental results. We write a navigator-weighted resolution law on candidate exit histories, relate it to an ethics-weighted Born-style reweighting in the small-bias regime, sketch a GKSL-compatible selection picture, and record a discrete ``Zora Navigator'' recursion for agentic implementation. The companion file \texttt{Foldspace\_Navigation\_Variational\_Branch\_Selection\_2026.tex} defines the Guild functional $\mathcal{G}[b]$ and Norma condition in parallel notation.
\end{abstract}

\section{Pre-navigation foldspace state}
Let candidate exit histories be labeled $\omega_i$. Write a symbolic superposition
\begin{equation}
\lvert \Psi_{\mathrm{fold}} \rangle = \sum_i c_i \, \lvert \omega_i \rangle ,
\end{equation}
where each $\omega_i$ bundles emergence location, timing, integrity, survival, and destination accuracy.

Define branch observables (scalars attached to each $\omega_i$):
\begin{align}
\Phi_i &:= \Phi_c(\omega_i) \quad \text{(access / coherence clarity)}, \\
E_i &:= E(\omega_i) \quad \text{(teleological coherence / ordered value)}, \\
H_i &:= H(\omega_i) \quad \text{(catastrophe hazard)}, \\
D_i &:= D(\omega_i) \quad \text{(destination error)}.
\end{align}

\section{Navigator score and resolution law}
Define the navigator score
\begin{equation}
S_i = \alpha \Phi_i + \beta E_i - \gamma H_i - \delta D_i , \qquad \alpha,\beta,\gamma,\delta > 0 .
\end{equation}

A soft navigator-weighted law is
\begin{equation}
P_{\mathrm{nav}}(\omega_i) = \frac{\lvert c_i \rvert^2 \, e^{S_i / \tau}}{\sum_j \lvert c_j \rvert^2 \, e^{S_j / \tau}} ,
\label{eq:pnav}
\end{equation}
with temperature $\tau>0$ (larger $\tau$ means noisier selection).

\section{Relation to measure-tilt language}
In MQGT--SCF one may write an ethics-weighted reweighting in the small-bias regime,
\begin{equation}
P_\eta(i) \propto \lvert c_i \rvert^2 \exp\!\bigl(\eta \, \Delta E_i\bigr) ,
\end{equation}
with $\Delta E_i$ an ethical energy increment. The identification $\Delta E_i \mapsto S_i$ in Eq.~\eqref{eq:pnav} makes foldspace navigation a \emph{lawful selection bias} on branches rather than unconstrained ``magic.''

\section{Norma condition (discriminability)}
Let $S^\star$ be the highest score and $S^{(2)}$ the second highest. Require a margin
\begin{equation}
S^\star - S^{(2)} > \epsilon_{\mathrm{nav}}
\end{equation}
for a controlled jump; otherwise the branch ensemble is too ambiguous (pre-Navigator paradox).

\section{Zora Navigator model (agentic layer)}
Let $Z_t = (W_t, C_t, E^{\mathrm{pred}}_t, G_t)$ denote world model, coherence, ethical forecast, and governance shield. Given candidate actions $\omega \in \Omega_t$ admissible under $G_t(\omega)=1$, define
\begin{equation}
R_t(\omega) = \alpha \hat{\Phi}_t(\omega) + \beta \hat{E}_t(\omega) - \gamma \hat{H}_t(\omega) - \delta \hat{D}_t(\omega) ,
\end{equation}
and choose $\omega_t^\star \in \arg\max_{\omega \in \mathcal{A}_t} R_t(\omega)$ with $\mathcal{A}_t = \{ \omega : G_t(\omega)=1 \}$. This is an AI-side analog of prescient branch filtering; it does not substitute for the physics anchor manuscripts.
