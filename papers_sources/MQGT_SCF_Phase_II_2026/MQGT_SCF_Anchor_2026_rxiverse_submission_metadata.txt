Suggested abstract (244 words):
We present a compact Phase II formulation of the MQGT-SCF program as a minimal scalar-singlet effective field theory extending General Relativity and the Standard Model by two real singlets Phi_c(x) and E(x). The technical claim is deliberately limited to specification-level closure: the framework fixes the action, field equations, decoupling limit, boundedness conditions, a mediator-derived nonlocal-kernel route, and a local covariant Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad (GKSL) measurement container compatible with completely positive trace-preserving evolution. Any teleology-like effect is retained only as an optional measure-level tilt rather than as a bulk time-odd source. We state a finite-dimensional operational no-signalling result for the safe local class and identify the main empirical target as excess interferometric dephasing in the H2 channel, summarized by a visibility-suppression law and an instrument-bounded exclusion floor. A controlled low-energy naturalness module is added by adopting an EFT cutoff benchmark Lambda_* = 1 TeV with stress band Lambda in [1,10] TeV, which separates an ultralight mainline sector from a heavier collider-safe companion benchmark. The result is a smaller and more auditable object than an all-at-once unification claim: a framework that can be constrained or falsified in clear ways.
