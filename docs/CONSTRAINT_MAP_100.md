## Interpretation

The experiment did not collapse the theory space. It removed a layer of strong claims and left behind a smaller, more subtle set of possibilities. That is exactly what early experiments are supposed to do.

**Not ruled out:** The universe loops. The universe spirals to Omega.  
**Ruled out:** Strong consciousness–QRNG coupling at the tested scale.  
**Tightened:** The search space.
