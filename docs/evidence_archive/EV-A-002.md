## Archived abstract excerpt

The paper derives fifth-force bounds using lunar laser ranging, Cassini, and Mercury precession data, showing that lunar laser ranging provides the strongest large-scale constraint and that the model approaches GR in the limit of sufficiently small fifth-force strength.
