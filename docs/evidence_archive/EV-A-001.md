## Archived abstract excerpt

Observed cooling of nearby isolated neutron stars is used to bound scalar fifth forces in the `10^-6` to `10^-12 m` range, excluding couplings down to approximately `g_N <= 5 x 10^-14` and superseding prior limits across six orders of magnitude in scalar mass. The source also reports Higgs-portal implications at the level of `sin(theta) <= 6 x 10^-11`.
