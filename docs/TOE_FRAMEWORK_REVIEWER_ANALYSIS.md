## 2. Where the Theory Aligns

### Scalar field additions

### Yukawa-type fifth-force
V(r) = -G m₁m₂/r (1 + α e^{-r/λ}). Standard beyond-SM test format. Eöt-Wash, atom interferometry, Casimir-scale tests. Empirical strategy is valid.

### Collapse reinterpretation
Family: GRW, CSL, Orch-OR. The model fits that lineage.

## 3. Where the Theory Diverges

### 3.1 Ethical scalar field E(x)
Physics usually avoids normative quantities. P(i) ∝ |c_i|² e^{-E/C} implies quantum outcomes depend on ethical value.

**Referee questions:**
- How is E defined operationally?
- How is it measured?
- What units does it have?

Until precise: reads as philosophical physics rather than testable physics.

### 3.2 Teleological Lagrangian term
Introduces directionality toward higher coherence/ethics. Breaks time-symmetric fundamental laws.

**Referee demands:**
- Proof this doesn't violate CPT
- Demonstration it doesn't allow signaling
- Derivation from deeper symmetry

### 3.3 Consciousness as fundamental field
Panpsychist models exist; none have experimental confirmation.

**Referee questions:**
- What couples Φc to matter?
- Why haven't we detected it?
- What energy scale?

Requires numerical answers.
